Landscaping & Yard Maintenance Provision

The property at 634 NE 28th Ave includes a front yard and backyard requiring regular upkeep. Tenants must select one of the following options and initial their choice below. This selection is binding for the duration of the tenancy. Failure to maintain selected option constitutes a lease violation.

Option A — Owner-Arranged Maintenance (+$125/month)

Owner/Agent will arrange a licensed landscaping service to maintain the front and back yard on a regular schedule. An additional $125/month will be added to the base rent, bringing total monthly rent to $4,120/month. This is at or below the low end of typical Portland landscaping service costs for a property of this size.

Option B — Tenant Self-Managed Maintenance (no additional charge)

Tenants elect to manage front and back yard maintenance independently, using their own landscaper or performing upkeep themselves. This option requires ongoing proof of compliance: tenants must provide documentation of professional service (receipts, invoices, or service agreements) or photographic evidence of maintained yards upon Owner/Agent request, and no less than once per quarter. If the yard is found to be in an unmaintained condition, Owner/Agent reserves the right to arrange a one-time cleanup at a flat fee of $250 per cleanup incident, billed directly to the tenant.

By initialing, you confirm you have read and selected your landscaping option above.
