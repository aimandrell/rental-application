Additional Information

Portland: Evictions older than 5 years cannot be used as a basis for denial. Evictions dismissed or ruled in tenant's favor cannot be considered. COVID-period evictions (Apr 2020–Mar 2022) cannot be considered.

Portland: Misdemeanors older than 3 years and felonies older than 7 years generally cannot be used for denial. Crimes no longer illegal in Oregon cannot be considered. An individualized assessment is required before denial based on criminal history.
